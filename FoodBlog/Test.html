Lemon Herb Chicken with Roasted Veggies

By Marin · 5 ingredients · 4.8★
Show ingredients & steps

Ingredients: chicken breast, lemon, rosemary, thyme, mixed root vegetables, olive oil, salt.

  1. Preheat oven to 220°C (425°F).
  2. Toss veggies with oil, roast 25–30 min.
  3. Sear chicken 2–3 min per side, finish in oven with lemon slices 8–10 min.
